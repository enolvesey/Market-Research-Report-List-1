<p><ul><li>Single Drum Road Roller</li><li>Double Drum Road Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small road rollers market mainly includes two types: single drum and double drum road rollers. Single drum road rollers feature one large drum at the front and wheels at the back, ideal for compacting soil and gravel on uneven terrains. In contrast, double drum road rollers are equipped with two drums, providing uniform compaction over different surfaces, making them suitable for asphalt paving. Both types are essential in construction and road maintenance, enhancing efficiency and productivity in various applications.</p></p>
