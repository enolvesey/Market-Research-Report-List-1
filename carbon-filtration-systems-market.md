<p><p>Carbon filtration systems primarily include granular activated carbon (GAC) and block carbon filters. GAC systems utilize loose granules of activated carbon to adsorb contaminants from air or water, effectively removing impurities and odors. They are versatile and commonly used in various applications. Block carbon filters, on the other hand, consist of compressed activated carbon in a solid block form, providing higher filtration efficiency and longer lifespan. Both types effectively enhance water quality and are essential in purification processes across residential and industrial sectors.</p></p>
