<p><ul><li>Rear Tow Seeder Bins</li><li>Front Mounted Bins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air seeder market is primarily divided into two types: rear tow seeder bins and front-mounted bins. Rear tow seeder bins are designed to be towed behind tractors, allowing for large capacity and efficient seed distribution over broad fields. In contrast, front-mounted bins attach to the front of tractors, offering enhanced visibility and maneuverability, particularly in tight spaces. Both market types cater to different farming needs, optimizing seed application for improved crop yields and operational efficiency.</p></p>
