<p><ul><li>Single Stage Pump</li><li>Multiple Stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inline process pump market features two primary types: single stage pumps and multiple stage pumps. Single stage pumps consist of one impeller, providing high flow rates and simpler design, making them suitable for low to moderate pressure applications. In contrast, multiple stage pumps contain two or more impellers, enabling them to generate higher pressures, ideal for applications requiring significant elevation or pressure boosts. Both types serve various industries, including chemical processing, water treatment, and oil and gas, catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
