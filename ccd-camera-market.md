<p><strong>The CCD Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD camera market is primarily divided into two types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture a full image in a single exposure, making them ideal for applications requiring high-resolution images of stationary subjects, such as in security and robotics. In contrast, line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, which is advantageous for inspecting continuous materials like webs or conveyor belts in manufacturing. Both types serve distinct needs across various industries.</p></p>
