<p><p>Lithium battery protection chips are essential components for ensuring the safety and longevity of lithium-ion batteries. The market is typically categorized into three types: Over Voltage (OV) protection chips, which prevent the battery from exceeding its maximum voltage; Over Temperature (OT) protection chips, which safeguard against excessive temperature conditions; and Over Current Discharge (OCD) protection chips, which halt current flow during excessive discharge. Together, these chips enhance battery performance and prevent potential hazards, promoting safe usage in various applications.</p></p>
