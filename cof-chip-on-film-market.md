<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Double Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chip-On-Film (COF) market consists of two primary types: Single Layer and Double Layer. Single Layer COF technology involves attaching a semiconductor chip directly onto a flexible film substrate, optimizing space and reducing manufacturing costs. In contrast, Double Layer COF incorporates two layers of film, allowing for enhanced electrical performance and improved thermal management. This dual-layer configuration supports complex applications and increases reliability, making it suitable for advanced electronic devices such as smartphones, tablets, and wearables.</p></p>
